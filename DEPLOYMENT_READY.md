## 🎓 LESSONS LEARNED

1. **Specificity > Generality**
   - "Save time" is weak → "Reclaim 1 hour/week" is strong

2. **Comparison > Claims**
   - "Good service" is weak → "Better than Poppins" is strong

3. **Value Framing > Price**
   - "$3/kg" feels expensive → "$15/week = <1 hour your salary" is compelling

4. **Trust Signals > Words**
   - "We're trustworthy" → Add badges, guarantees, and proof

5. **Social Proof > Everything**
   - "As Seen In" media logos increase credibility 20%+

6. **FAQ > Sales Copy**
   - Answer objections before they're raised = conversions
