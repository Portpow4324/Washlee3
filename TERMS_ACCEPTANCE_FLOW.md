## ✨ Key Points Highlighted

### 1️⃣ Terms Checkbox Section
- **PROMINENT**: Highlighted mint/primary box draws attention
- **CLEAR**: Large checkbox with bold label text
- **ACTIONABLE**: Direct links to Terms and Privacy Policy
- **CONFIRMATION**: Green "✓ Terms accepted" shows success

### 2️⃣ Terms Modal
- **ACCESSIBLE**: Large modal (90vh) ensures all content visible
- **VISUAL**: Gradient header makes it feel premium
- **TRACKED**: Progress bar + percentage shows scroll completion
- **MANDATORY**: Accept button disabled until scrolled to bottom
- **CLEAR**: "Scroll to Bottom to Accept" → "✓ I Accept & Agree"

### 3️⃣ Validation Helper
- **HELPFUL**: Shows exactly what's missing
- **SPECIFIC**: "✗ Accept Terms & Conditions (clickable link)"
- **CONTEXTUAL**: Only shows on Step 0 when form invalid
- **ACTIONABLE**: Link opens modal directly

### 4️⃣ Smart Routing
- **INTELLIGENT**: Detects if user already logged in
- **CONVENIENT**: Auto-fills data for returning customers
- **CLEAR**: "Already have account?" link works both ways
